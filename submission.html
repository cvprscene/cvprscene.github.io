Call for Extended Abstracts

We invite submissions of extended abstracts describing work in 3D scene understanding including but not limited to the following topics:

  • Large-scale 3D scene datasets
  • 3D object modeling and analysis
  • 3D scene rendering and synthesis
  • 3D scene parsing and reconstruction
  • 3D human pose estimation and 3D activity reasoning
  • Robotics applications and 3D interactive agents
  • 3D visual common sense reasoning of geometry and affordances
  • Physical 3D scene understanding (realizability, object stability, activity risks)
  • Task-oriented 3D scene understanding

Submission should be up to 4 pages (including references) in CVPR19 format. The workshop committee will slightly review the abstracts in a single-blind fashion. We encourage submissions of works that have been previously published or are to be presented in the main conference.

All accepted abstracts will be included in a poster presentation session. The best abstracts will be invited to give an spotlight or oral presentation. The accepted abstracts will be archived by the CVF.

Please submit the abstracts to cvpr.3dscene@gmail.com.
